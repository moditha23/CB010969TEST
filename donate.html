Help Estate Workers

Tea plantation labourers from India are still at the bottom of Sri Lankan society. Many of them continue to labour in deplorable conditions for pitiful daily wages. Sri Lanka, according to the Planters' Association of Ceylon, pays its tea workers more than other major tea producing countries such as China, India, and Kenya. Nonetheless, the tea workers remain impoverished. Tea workers have attempted to reach collective agreements with Sri Lankan authorities on several occasions, with little success. Workers have organised rallies to demand a fair salary, fed up with stagnating earnings and continual increases in the cost of living. So please help these people with your kind donations.
